About Owl's Nest

Owlâ€™s Nest

is an informative website made for research location convenience. This idea was proposed in consideration of the many students who struggle to locate a place fit for group tasks. The website provides:

  • Names of Co-working spaces.
  • Address of the space.
  • Price rates of the space.
  • Description of the space.
  • Amenities of the space.
  • Pictures of the space

Ordering System

The style of order in which the locations of co-working spaces would be seen, can be changed with the click of a button. Specifically, there are two orders to choose from: the ascending order of Price rates or the descending order of recommendation rates.

Customer Rating System

Owls Nest will have a Customer Rating system, where the users who have visited a place of their choice can leave a review on this site. They can tell their satisfaction by leaving a comment and giving them owls; these are alternate star ratings of 1 to 5.

Alternative Inquiry

For users who require further inquiry and information for the locations of their choice. The website provides each the contacts of the locations of their choice. These contacts, when clicked, are programmed to bring them to the webpage of the location they picked.

Location Marking

After picking the user's preferred co-working space, the website can serve as a GPS to guide the user using Google Maps to track the establishment from their specific location.
